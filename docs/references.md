# References for Understanding the Model and Data

## 1. Bayesian Change Point Modeling
- **Adams, R.P., & MacKay, D.J.C. (2007).** "Bayesian Online Changepoint Detection." arXiv:0710.3742 [https://arxiv.org/abs/0710.3742]  
  *Reviewed for understanding the fundamentals of Bayesian change point detection.*

- **PyMC Documentation – Change Points Tutorial**  
  [https://docs.pymc.io/en/stable/pymc-examples/examples/time_series/change_points.html](https://docs.pymc.io/en/stable/pymc-examples/examples/time_series/change_points.html)  
  *Step-by-step guide on implementing discrete change points in PyMC.*

## 2. Time Series Analysis
- **Hyndman, R.J., & Athanasopoulos, G. (2021).** *Forecasting: Principles and Practice, 3rd edition.* OTexts. [https://otexts.com/fpp3/](https://otexts.com/fpp3/)  
  *Covered trend, seasonality, stationarity, and volatility analysis.*

- **Box, G.E.P., Jenkins, G.M., & Reinsel, G.C. (2015).** *Time Series Analysis: Forecasting and Control.* Wiley.  
  *Reviewed for classical statistical approaches to time series modeling.*

## 3. Statistical Testing & EDA
- **Statsmodels Documentation – Augmented Dickey-Fuller Test**  
  [https://www.statsmodels.org/stable/generated/statsmodels.tsa.stattools.adfuller.html](https://www.statsmodels.org/stable/generated/statsmodels.tsa.stattools.adfuller.html)  
  *Used to check stationarity of Brent oil log returns.*

## 4. Data Sources
- **Historical Brent Oil Prices Dataset** – Kaggle  
  [https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/datasets/brent-oil-prices](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/datasets/brent-oil-prices)
